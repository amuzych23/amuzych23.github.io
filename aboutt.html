Hey, I’m Anna Muzychenko!

Photography has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. It all started with capturing family gatherings and snapping portraits of my friends—just for fun. But over time, I realized how much I loved capturing real, meaningful moments through my lens.

Now, at 18, I’m growing my portfolio and turning this passion into something bigger. I focus on personal photoshoots, event photography, and outdoor/nature shots—anything that lets me capture people, emotions, and the beauty of the world around us.

Originally from Ukraine, I’m currently living in Lithuania, studying communication and exploring the creative side of visual storytelling. Whether it’s a personal portrait or a vibrant event, my goal is to create photos that evoke emotion and capture the essence of the moment.

If you’re looking for authentic, storytelling-driven photography, let’s make it happen. Feel free to explore my work and reach out—I’d love to bring your vision to life!
